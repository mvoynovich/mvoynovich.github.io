Lab 3: Build a Website

Intro ITWS

Create a personal Intro to ITWS website and deploy it to your personal web servers.

  • The web site will be placed in the "iit" subdirectory of your Azure instance. Refer to the tutorials from Lab 1 to get started.
  • Guidelines:
    • You must have at least two pages: home and projects. Name these files:
      • index.html (this will be your landing page, or home page)
      • projects.html (this will be the main page to introduce your labs and should reside in a folder other than your iit root. This should be according to your IA
    • You must have a consistent header, menu, content area, and footer across your pages.
    • The only file in your root (iit) folder (at the root of your site) is the index.html page (.htaccess, README.md, and any other files from lab1)
    • All resources (e.g. CSS files) should be kept in a separate area(s) as per your design.
    • Populate your projects page with links to the labs, beginning with your resume; continue to do this as we move forward this semester.
    • As covered in class, your code must be valid and properly indented. (And don't forget to set the <title> tag on each page.)
    • NOTE: You already have an index.html page from Lab 1. You will edit this file to make it more suited to your personal 'brand'
  • NOTE: While not required for Lab 3 - you may want to start this lab by creating a branch called lab 3 and working from there.
  • NOTE: At this point you must have security working for you iit pages on your site. If the pages are not secured you will lose points
  • We will work through some of this together in class.
  • You will graded using the following as a guideline. Creativity will be rewarded.
    • Validity : / 20
    • Indentation : / 10
    • Completeness : / 50
    • Quality/creativity : / 20
  • I have included an HTML template to help you get started: HTML5 template
  • Feel free to experiment and be creative - and use the web as a reference!! (e.g. W3Schools)

  • This lab is due by the date on the LMS assigment panel It must be posted to your Azure instance/Personal website located at FQDN/iit/index.html.

    Please update your README.md file in your root directory and with comments about the lab, justifications for your chosen methods/styles/tech, etc. In the file, include the correct URL for your site. (remove any password entries from Lab 1)

    Include your outlines that you created in the IA class.

    By the time you complete this lab, you should have a copy of your iit folder;

    • on your local machine for editing
    • in your GitHub repositories
    • on your Azure instance

    Create a zip file of your iit directory, name it - yourRCSid-ITWS1100-Lab3.zip and submit it to the Assignments area of LMS. (it will contain your entire repository, with your HTML, CSS, README, and any other files/resources relevant to your site)

    Include a working URL to your iit homepage in your README files.
